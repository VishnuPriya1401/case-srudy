Asian Series OTT App

Why AsianBird App?

This is an OTT application for Asian shows like Korean, Mandarin, Thai, and Japanese series and movies. It provides users with an easy search option and allows users to filter results according to their preferred language and genre. This app has a Wishlist feature, which allows users to save their favorite shows.

This app targets a range of users who use smart devices to watch Asian shows like series and movies in Korean, Mandarin, and Thai. It also aims at users who love to set specific genre options while searching for their favorite shows.
