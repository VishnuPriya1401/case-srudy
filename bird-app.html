Birds Information App

Goal and Targeted Audience

This is an informative application that helps users learn about birds of all varieties. It provides a clear search option that allows users to use either the photos, sound, or location to find information about the birds. It also comes with a save details feature for effortless responses to later searches.

This App target people who love nature, and who have interest to know about the livelihood of birds around them.
